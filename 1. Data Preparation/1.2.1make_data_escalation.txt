This R-file aggregated the data_icews_cm data to data_escalation. The events are categorised and are sumed up into 10 new variables. This variables are then added to the cm_data and the pgm_data. 


Generation of 3 new datasets
data_escalation: aggregated data_icews_cm
cm_icews_data: data_escalation + cm_data
pgm_icews_data: data_escalation + pgm_data
